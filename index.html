About Our Center

Our Mission

The DWU Center for Learning & Teaching is dedicated to fostering academic excellence by providing comprehensive support to both faculty and students. We serve as a hub for innovative teaching methodologies, learning resources, and professional development opportunities.

Our Vision

To be a leading academic support center that transforms teaching and learning experiences through collaboration, innovation, and evidence-based practices.

Core Values

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Collaboration and community
  • Evidence-based practices

Our Services

Comprehensive support for both faculty and students to enhance teaching and learning experiences

  • Mentoring programs
  • Classroom observation & feedback
  • Curriculum design support

Technology Integration
